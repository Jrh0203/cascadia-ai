# ADR 0095: Frontier Observable Bypass Separability

Status: complete; rejected.

Date: 2026-06-16

Experiment ID: `complete-action-frontier-observable-bypass-v1`

## Context

ADR 0094 proved that neither a linear nor a one-hidden-layer head can recover
the frontier target from the selected model's final 192-dimensional candidate
embedding. Train target recall reached only 22.48% and 24.67%, with zero exact
sets. The output representation has lost target structure, but exact
model-visible input collisions remain zero from ADR 0090.

The next question is whether the original observable action and prior features
remain separable, either alone or when combined with board context from the
frozen embedding. This localizes the smallest honest representation change
before replacing the complete trunk.

## Frozen Inputs

- Reuse ADR 0094's exact train and validation embedding caches.
- Decode the original 140 action features and eight observable prior features
  from the unchanged train and validation datasets.
- Raw feature width: 148 float32 values.
- Combined feature width: 192 frozen embedding plus 148 raw values = 340.
- Include every open action exactly once and preserve ADR 0094 group order.
- Verify every group offset and action hash against the embedding cache.
- No teacher, label, target, dataset, or selector change.

The raw values are observable at inference. They contain no R600, R1200,
R4800, frontier membership, source provenance, or selected-winner label.

## Sidecar Throughput

All four Macs already hold the immutable datasets and embedding caches. Each
host independently materializes the deterministic raw sidecar locally. This
is intentional duplication of cheap CPU preprocessing to avoid repeating ADR
0094's 2.26 GiB network relay. The per-file payload identity must be
bit-identical across hosts even though host/timing metadata differs.

No host repeats neural trunk inference.

## Frozen Probes

All probes use ADR 0094's group-balanced binary objective and unchanged
frontier-anchored width-64 selector. They train for 20 epochs and select by
train target recall, train exact sets, then validation recall.

## Classification Gates

1. `raw_linear_bypass_sufficient`
   - raw-linear train recall at least 60%; and
   - raw-linear exact train sets at least 5%.
2. `raw_nonlinear_bypass_sufficient`
   - linear gate fails;
   - raw-nonlinear train recall at least 80%;
   - raw-nonlinear exact train sets at least 25%;
   - validation recall at least 50%; and
   - validation exact sets at least 1%.
3. `combined_bypass_sufficient`
   - both raw-only gates fail;
   - combined train recall at least 80%;
   - combined exact train sets at least 25%;
   - validation recall at least 50%; and
   - validation exact sets at least 1%.
4. `combined_bypass_train_separable_not_generalized`
   - combined train gates pass but validation transfer fails.
5. `observable_bypass_insufficient`
   - combined train gates fail.

Every report additionally requires complete finite scoring, exact group/action
totals, RSS below 4 GiB, zero process swaps, and sealed test unopened.

## Consequences

- A raw-only pass authorizes the smallest observable bypass head.
- A combined-only pass authorizes an embedding-plus-raw residual bypass.
- A train-only pass requires generalization work before deployment.
- Combined train failure authorizes a new trunk representation and closes
  further head-only treatments.

No gameplay or promotion follows directly from this audit.

## Maximum Compute

Four deterministic local sidecar generations, three independent one-seed
probe jobs, cross-host replay, and correctness/reporting work. No trunk
inference, full-network training, sweep, extra seed, teacher compute, sealed
test, gameplay, cloud, or external compute.

## Result

All three probes failed their preregistered fit gates. Raw linear reached
26.31% train target recall, raw nonlinear reached 28.35%, and the combined
embedding-plus-raw probe reached 30.50%. Each recovered only 0.18% of train
target sets exactly. Validation recall remained 26.04%-26.24%, with zero exact
sets.

All four hosts used the same 95-file MLX runtime bundle and independently
generated byte-identical raw sidecars. john4 reproduced every saved probe's
scientific payload exactly. Maximum RSS was 3.07 GB, process swaps were zero,
and the sealed domains remained unopened.

The classification is `observable_bypass_insufficient`. Candidate-wise
bypass heads, output-head changes, and further objective treatments are
closed. The next authorized mechanism is a preregistered fork of distinct
linear-memory candidate-relative trunk representations. Replication remains
closed until one mechanism passes its train and validation gates.
